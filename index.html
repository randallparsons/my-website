Approach the mysteries of the Guitar with Confidence

Ever wonder how some musicians make the guitar look so easy? No doubt it comes down to a lot of practice, but what about the "how?" of it all?  Wouldn't it seem a lot easier if there was a simple way to communicate the complexities of the musical aspects of guitar with a simple visual language? Well guess what?

...There is!

It can be as EASY as 1 * 2 * 3:

  1. Learn basics of reading tab within minutes!
  2. Practice translating to finger positions on the fretboard...
  3. Clamp down tight, fingers perpendicular to fretboard, & practice picking notes clearly and strumming the whole chord!

Benefits of Knowing Tablature

  • Guitar tab is universally understood among musicians the world over!
  • Trade your latest tricks and discoveries with your friends!
  • Open up the world of free online versions of all your favorite songs!
